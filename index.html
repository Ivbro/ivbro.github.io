About me

Hi, I am Ivo Léon, MSc Industrial Design graduate of Eindhoven University of Technology. Furthermore, I am a designer specializing in concept development and evaluation, through product design and user research.

I focus on creating value through the design of impactful products. Through a user-centered approach and a dedication to quality I aim to design physical electronic products that provide great value for the end-user. With a critical mindset and holistic view, I apply my broad knowledge to be an effective designer.
‍
Read more about my vision
My main focus is the concept development and evaluation stage of the design process. Through technical skills I am able to design and realize prototypes and work together with engineering experts. I involving users all throughout the process and conduct structured user research and evaluation. This aims to ensure that the envisioned value matches their needs and wishes, as well as generating insights to steer future development.

Calmscape

A design project about a dynamic artwork in hospital waiting rooms. Through subtle movement it distracts and relaxes patients in busy and chaotic emergency rooms.

Evaluating Stress Management Practices

A research project about the evaluation of stress management practices. It compares two methods of creating stress awareness and stimulating stress management technique usage.

Ludis: Increasing Fitness Guidance

A design project that revolves around increasing fitness guidance. It introduces a system that allows users to call over trainers, lowering the barriers for asking for help.

LSG ATOM: A modular build kit for sensing devices

A design project about the design of a modular build kit for sensing devices. The plug-and-play system allows users to create and deploy sensing devices for rapid prototyping and evaluation.
